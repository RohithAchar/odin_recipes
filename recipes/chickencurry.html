Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken
  • Cooking oil
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Tomato
  • Curry powder
  • Salt
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes soft and lightly golden, which usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan and sauté for another 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and start to break down, typically for about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in the curry powder and cook for a couple of minutes to release its flavors.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add a little water (about 1/4 to 1/2 cup) to create a curry sauce. Stir well to combine, and then cover the pan. Let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender. If the curry gets too dry, you can add more water.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt as needed.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  9. Serve the chicken curry hot with rice or flatbread.
